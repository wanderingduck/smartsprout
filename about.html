About


Having worked in digital marketing for the last decade and I have literally worked on hundreds of websites. Yet, I became frustrated with the fact that, due to the high cost of digital agency support, smaller businesses and start-ups are left to fend for themselves.

The good news
As a business owner, there has never been a time when it was easier to connect to your market and setting up has never been cheaper. Cheap website hosting, free website builders (although I wouldn't recommend them) and direct market access through YouTube, Facebook, Instagram and Twitter. 

The bad news
Unfortunately, there are so many more competitors in the market with all the same tools and opportunities as you. Not to mention, Even if your product or service is ideal for your customer, there has never been a harder time in history to get people to care.

This should make you feel better
I will teach you everything you need to know about attracting the attention of your market online, improving sales conversions and how to use social media to gain an advantage. The best part: I will do it in a way that keeps your costs to an absolute minimum. I will always aim to work within your budget and I expect you will find my costs more than reasonable.
